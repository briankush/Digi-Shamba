About E-Shamba

With the dificulties faced by the use of analog and outdated farm management methods and record keeping, the demand for a digitized farm management is no doubt a key essential in the current evolving technological world. This is what led to the rise of E-Shamba, a platform aiming to help farmers with or without diverse farming knowledge easily handle all the farm activities.

Features

  • Task Management
  • Digitized record keeping
  • Weather Updates
  • Market Prices
